About My Research

For over 11 years, I have dedicated my career to advancing agricultural entomology and integrated pest management, with a specialized focus on the unique farming ecosystems of Northeast India.

My research journey began with an M.Sc. in Agricultural Entomology, followed by a Ph.D. in Integrated Pest Management, where I developed expertise in ecological engineering approaches including habitat manipulation, trap cropping, and natural enemy conservation.

Currently serving as a Technical Officer under the Gramin Krishi Mausam Sewa (GKMS) at ICAR Research Complex for NEH Region, Kolasib, Mizoram, I integrate agrometeorological data with pest surveillance to provide farmers with actionable, location-specific agricultural advisories.
